What Efficiency Center is about?

Efficiency Center is a meeting point for those looking for workspaces from all business fields. It offers various options for tenants, such as open workspaces, dedicated workspaces, and group offices, these spaces provide dedicated desks within a secure communal area, shared by several different individual workers or businesses. From daily drop-ins to monthly and yearly membership, there is a wide range of leasing choices available depending on your needs.

What are the features of Efficiency Center?

  • 24/7 Reception
  • Open workspaces, individual and group offices, meeting, and conference room: Stylish/Modern coworking space, fully furnished, pre-cabled, with coffee, and tea/ Barista service.
  • Seating area with an astonishing view.
  • You can book the meeting and conference room and open spaces by hour/day.
  • Efficiency Center offers four options of subscription: daily, monthly, quarterly, biannually, and annually.
  • Our offices provide flexible short-term leases, which help you to keep your budget in check. When leasing a space like this, you only pay to use it at a time that suits you.

What are our services?

Our main services: Open workspaces, dedicated workspaces, individual offices, group offices, meeting room and conference room.
